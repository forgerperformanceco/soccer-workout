Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026 · Plain-English, because it should be.

Yardsmith is a free golf training & nutrition app. We collect as little as possible, we don't sell your data, and you can delete it any time. Here's exactly what happens.

What's stored on your device

Everything you enter — your calculator inputs, food preferences, workout log, bodyweight and clubhead-speed history, and your settings — is saved locally in your browser (localStorage). If you never sign in, your data never leaves your device.

What's stored in the cloud (only if you sign in)

  • Your email address — used only to sign you in (a one-tap magic link, no password) and to identify your account.
  • A backup of your app data (the same inputs and logs above) so your progress follows you across devices.
  • Leaderboard entry — only if you choose to join: a display name/handle and your Yardsmith Score. You can leave the leaderboard at any time.

Sign-in and cloud storage are handled by Supabase, our backend provider.

The AI coach

When you ask the AI coach a question, your message and the relevant numbers from your own plan are sent to our server and to Anthropic (the AI provider) to generate a reply. We don't use your conversations to train models, and we don't sell them.

What we don't do

  • We don't sell or rent your personal data — ever.
  • We don't run third-party advertising trackers.
  • We don't ask for payment info (the app is free).

Deleting your data

On your device: clearing the app's site data (or your browser data) removes the local copy. For your cloud account and any leaderboard entry, email us and we'll delete everything tied to your account.

Health disclaimer

Yardsmith provides general, evidence-based fitness and nutrition starting points for education — it is not medical advice. Talk to a qualified professional before starting any new training or nutrition program.
